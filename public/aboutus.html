About us

Established in 1989, Almost Christmas Prop Shoppe is one of world's largest holiday prop houses. We continue to be the only prop house in Hollywood dedicated exclusively to Christmas, having worked with industry leading production companies, independent studios, and numerous businesses. Located in the heart of North Hollywood, Almost Christmas Prop Shoppe maintains a vast inventory of holiday props including trees, wreaths, garlands, mantels, sleighs, tabletop décor, specialty lights, lawn pieces, glass and non-breakable ornaments, and many more. Our warehouse also contains authentic gingerbread houses and winter wonderland displays, which have been featured in countless film and television productions. In addition to our extensive inventory of holiday props, we also offer personalized holiday designs and onsite decorating through our in-house design team, Santa Clause Productions.
